Why is APS the Best Choice for Waste Management?

  • Complete Combustion: Convert waste into environmentally friendly CO2 and H2O.
  • Plasma Technology: Burns waste at high temperatures (700–1000°C) with minimal smoke.
  • Fuel Efficiency: Utilizes used oil and water as efficient, eco-friendly fuel alternatives.
  • Types of Waste: Plastic, paper, aluminum foil, food scraps, wet, dry, wood, cloth, styrofoam, diapers, pads, livestock waste, and even glass. Excludes metal and stones.
  • Versatile: Suitable for households, mosques, villas, offices, schools, residential areas, medium-scale factories, etc.!
  • Zero Waste System: Highly efficient with only 1% ash residue.
  • Economic Recycling: Enables recycling with economic value up to 20%.
  • Warranty & Support: 6-month factory warranty and custom capacity options.
